about me

In my early working life, I delved into the film and broadcasting industry by writing promo scripts and film treatments for international film producers engaged in co-productions with National Geographic, The Discovery Channel, Canal+ and Animal Planet. I was groomed under the expert tutelage of an acclaimed Italian film producer, Stefania Soncini Lamberti.

My repertoire in broadcasting extends to mini-documentaries, corporate videos and local TV productions including production assistance on an award-winning sitcom titled The Coconuts (Season 1). My latest love is web development, where I coloasce all the skills from my broadcasting background into the dynamic, creative, and exciting world of web development. I can make your website dazzle and sizzle like a blockbuster!
